At Zedd, we are an avid group of developers who code for fun. We Strive to provide gamers a good website that gives them access to many games. We are gamers ourselves so we know what it is like when you're bored and have no games to play. Our mission is to never be bored and make a website where you never run out of games to play. This project is not meant to copy others, but to expand upon their ideas, and give a platform to support them. The team that coded is Abhiraam, Adarsh, and Daksh. Abhiraam is doing the HTML, Adarsh is doing CSS, while Daksh is doing the Javascript. Also, our tester is Aden, and huge props to him for helping. As we all know at Zedd, no product is finished or perfect, so be on the lookout for patch notes every now and then. Have Fun!
